Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the things people ask most.

Is AI Quick Translate free to use?

Yes. The app is free to download and includes a generous daily allowance of translation credits. A premium subscription is available to remove ads and unlock higher usage limits.

What file formats can I translate?

AI Quick Translate handles PDF, Microsoft Word (DOCX), PowerPoint (PPTX), OpenDocument (ODT), EPUB e-books, plain text files, audio files (MP3, AAC, WAV), and images captured by camera or selected from your gallery.

How many languages does the app support?

The app supports translation between 100+ languages — English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Turkish, Russian, Hindi, and many more.

Does the app work offline?

AI Quick Translate uses cloud-based AI models for the highest translation quality, so an internet connection is required. Your translation history is stored on your device and remains accessible offline.

Is my data used to train AI models?

No. We do not use any of the content you submit (text, audio, documents, or images) to train AI models. Original files are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of processing.

Can I translate full PDF and Word documents?

Yes. Upload a PDF or Office document and either translate the entire file or pick specific pages or sections. The translated file is delivered back in the same format.

How does live voice conversation translation work?

Pick two languages, tap the microphone on the side that's speaking, and the app transcribes and translates the speech in real time — perfect for back-and-forth conversation across a language barrier.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from Google Play Store → Account → Payments & subscriptions, or from the app's side menu by tapping Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
